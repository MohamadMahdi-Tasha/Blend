The Blend

Established in 2017 by a creative and passionate team of architects and interior designers, Dubai based blend studio has built a significant portfolio of luxury residential and commercial projects, projects that reflect a wide and diverse client base. Our philosophy in design revolves around innovating and preserving the world around us through thoughtful and inspired design. Our goal is to listen to clients, understand and predict their needs and provide exceptional design solutions . Our core values are and will remain professionalism , collaboration , friendship and transparency. We expanded our operations to the North America market in 2020 with headquarter in Vancouver and a team in Dubai and Rome. blend platform was founded in 2020 as an excuse to travelling the globe and uncovering hidden talents from all over the world and exhibiting their creations.An online platform devoted to design, architecture and fashion.We want you to find that piece that makes your space more special than ever before. Whether it’s a handmade ceramic from Poland, white Volakis marble from Greece, or bronze statue from Persia, all of our designs are created with love, care and expert craftsmanship. We present new collections on invitation-only popup shows "Bonafide" seasonally in Vancouver. Selected pieces will be available on our online shop. Bonta is another addition where we share our reciepies with our clients.
